OneGov Strategy Overview

Strategic Context

The OneGov Strategy is GSA's bold initiative aimed at modernizing how the federal government purchases goods and services. Starting with commercial software, this governmentwide approach consolidates purchasing power to achieve better pricing, simplified access, and improved security compliance across all federal agencies.

  • Consolidated Purchasing Power: Leverage the government's collective buying power to negotiate better deals
  • Simplified Access: Make it easier for federal customers to browse and redeem negotiated agreements
  • Enhanced Security: Ensure all solutions meet federal security and compliance requirements
  • Cost Savings: Achieve significant discounts through enterprise-wide agreements

Ready to learn more? Complete our industry questionnaire to stay informed about upcoming opportunities and participate in the OneGov Strategy initiative.

For Federal Agencies

Federal agencies can immediately begin taking advantage of current OneGov agreements:

  • Review Available Agreements: Browse the Current Agreements tab to see all available discounts and offerings
  • Contact ITVMO: Reach out to our team at itvmo@gsa.gov for guidance on which agreements best fit your needs
  • Access Through Existing Vehicles: Most OneGov agreements are available through GSA's Multiple Award Schedule and other established procurement vehicles
  • Stay Informed: Complete the industry questionnaire to receive updates on new agreements and opportunities

For Industry Partners

Industry partners interested in participating in future OneGov negotiations should:

  • Complete the Questionnaire: Submit the industry questionnaire to express interest and stay updated on opportunities
  • Maintain GSA Schedule Contracts: Ensure your organization has active GSA Multiple Award Schedule contracts
  • Demonstrate Government Experience: Highlight your track record serving federal agencies
  • Prepare for Enterprise Negotiations: Be ready to discuss government-wide pricing and terms
