What is OneGov?

GSA's OneGov Strategy is a comprehensive plan to transform how government buys almost everything. Instead of hundreds of separate agency contracts, we leverage the collective purchasing power of the entire federal government to secure unprecedented discounts while ensuring consistent security standards and simplified access.

Why OneGov IT?

OneGov IT is Phase 1 - focusing on software and technology where we can quickly achieve the greatest savings. Previously, agencies negotiated separate contracts for the same software, often paying premium prices while duplicating effort. OneGov IT eliminates this waste by leveraging our collective $50+ billion in annual federal IT spending to negotiate as one buyer.

  • Massive Cost Savings: Secure discounts of 70-90% on software your agency already uses from vendors like Microsoft, Adobe, Google, and Salesforce
  • Direct OEM Relationships: We work directly with original equipment manufacturers, eliminating markup and ensuring clear accountability for support and performance
  • Reduced Administrative Burden: Access pre-negotiated agreements through value added resellers on GSA's Multiple Award Schedule instead of running separate procurements
  • Consistent Security Standards: All agreements include standardized contract terms with consistent expectations around support, patching, and access controls

Do you represent a software OEM? Complete our industry questionnaire signup form to participate.

Questions? Our team at ITVMO@gsa.gov is here to help.

How to Participate

For Federal Agencies

Federal agencies can immediately begin taking advantage of current OneGov agreements:

  • Review Available Agreements: Browse the Current Agreements tab to see all available discounts and offerings
  • Contact ITVMO: Reach out to our team at itvmo@gsa.gov for guidance on which agreements best fit your needs
  • Access Through Existing Vehicles: Most OneGov agreements are available through GSA's Multiple Award Schedule and other established procurement vehicles
  • Stay Informed: Subscribe to ... to receive updates on new agreements and opportunities

For Industry Partners

Industry partners interested in participating in future OneGov negotiations should:

  • Complete the Questionnaire: Submit the industry questionnaire to express interest and stay updated on opportunities
  • Maintain GSA Schedule Contracts: Ensure your organization has active GSA Multiple Award Schedule contracts
  • Demonstrate Government Experience: Highlight your track record serving federal agencies
  • Prepare for Enterprise Negotiations: Be ready to discuss government-wide pricing and terms
