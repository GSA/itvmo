    IT Buyers Community of Practice

    Active Initiatives/Community Teams

    • Core-Workstation Category Team

      Maintains a framework and strategy to reduce costs and increase value through the application of strategic sourcing principles for desktops and laptops that meet the requirements of the Federal Government for the bulk of systems purchased and also incorporated best practices in spend management.

    • Cyber Category Team

      Assessing cyber offerings to advise agencies on their acquisition strategies and help guide compliance to the governing policies.

    • Emerging Tech Working Group

      Identifies governmentwide and agency specific standards for using emerging technologies like artificial intelligence (AI) and natural language processing (NLP) to revolutionize the federal procurement process.

    • FinOps Working Group

      Brings together constituents from the government to collect best practices and use cases and prepare recommendations for enhancing public sector cloud acquisition efficiency. Read about our successful work with the U.S. Army that saved millions.

  • CIO.gov Community of Practice

    CIO.gov Community of Practice

    Active Initiatives/Community Teams

    • Chief Information Security Officer Council

      Primary body for Federal interagency CISO collaboration and communication, working to continuously improve the security posture of the United States

    • Innovation Committee

      Responsible for identifying and analyzing emerging technology. Works to help agencies to speed the adoption of new practices, processes and technologies.

    • Enterprise Operations Committee

      Focuses on acquisition reform and Federal IT infrastructure developing strategies, policy advice in the areas of Federal Information Technology Acquisition Reform Act (FITARA), Technology Business Management (TBM), Data Center Optimization Initiative (DCOI), Capital Planning and Investment Control (CPIC), Information Resource Management (IRM), Enterprise Architecture (EA), Shared Services

    • Workforce Committee

      Focuses on the entire employee lifecycle. Working with this HR community on developing, implementing, and communicating strategies to recruit, retain, and manage a fully trained and qualified IT workforce for the current and future workplace.

    Communities of Practice (CoP)

    • Accessibility Community of Practice

      Acts as the principal interagency forum to support accessibility of information technology (IT) products and services in the Federal government. Works to develop accessibility standards for IT development and procurement.

    • Cloud and Infrastructure Community of Practice

      Facilitates information sharing among federal agencies on topics ranging from application rationalization, data center optimization, and federal cloud computing, helping agencies to speed up speed cloud migration and data center consolidation

    • Federal Mobility Group

      Works across the federal government to identify common mobility challenges, develop workable solutions, and create opportunities to share best practices.

    • Federal Technology Investment Management Community of Practice

      Works to mature the integration of Technology Business Management (TBM), IT Capital Planning, and IT Investment Control (CPIC) and portfolio management practices in the federal government through the sharing of best practices and lessons learned.

    • Small and Micro Agency CIO and CISO Council

      Focuses on the needs and unique issues facing small agency Chief Information Officers (CIOs) and Chief Information Security Officers (CISOs), promotes information sharing and acts to ensure small agencies insight and input is considered by OMB when developing policy and guidance.

  • Federal Program Management CoPs

    Federal Program Management CoPs

    Active Initiatives/Community Teams

    • Acquisition Project Management Working Group

      Addresses matters related to executive agencies acquiring (purchasing or leasing) goods and services, through the phases of need recognition and acquisition planning; contract formation; and contract administration.

    • Community of Practice for Contracting Officers

      Brings together Contracting Officer's Representatives from all agencies across the Federal Government facilitating an environment of networking to exchange information, leverage training resources, and develop careers in the aid of government-wide improvement of all phases of contract management.

    • Community of Practice for Contracting Officer’s Representatives

      Brings together Contracting Officer's Representatives from all agencies across the Federal Government facilitating an environment of networking to exchange information, leverage training resources, and develop careers in the aid of government-wide improvement of all phases of contract management.

    • Cross-Functional Working Group

      Aids in the sharing of best practices, tools, templates, guidance, and supporting resources, and forums for discussion on each of the five Project Management Phases – Initiate, Plan, Execute, Control and Close

    • Information Technology Working Group

      Promotes an interconnected, collaborative approach to review, discuss and address IT management and governance requirements to assist Federal Agencies in improving performance.

    • Professional Development Working Group

      Provides a forum within which members can network, engage, and share resources on the various aspects of learning and development needed for the federal P/PM profession.
